A true Tarot reading is best done with a Tarot practitioner. The story the cards tell by their relationship with each other contains most of the meaning. This site aims to teach students using the original interpretations given by A.E. Waite, but it is no substitute for a real reading. The author bears no responsibility for any decisions made.

Tarot practitioners are energy healers and therapists, who use a combination of the symbolism in the cards and intuition to divine meanings and insights. A computer or web program is never a substitute for this type of relationship. With that said, a basic familiarity with the RWS (Rider-Waite-Smith) Tarot is essential for any beginning Tarot student, or curious onlooker.

On this site, we use the Celtic Cross spread, which provides a wealth of information for any reader.

Before you draw, get grounded with a short meditation. Call for the beneficient spirits of your ancestors and the Earth to provide you with protection and truth. Then, when you are ready, click the button.
